Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

Artlyx ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information when you use it.

Information We Collect

Artlyx does not collect, store, or transmit personal information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Messages

Network Speed Monitoring

The App monitors network traffic statistics available on your device in order to display real-time network speed information. This information is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS – To display ongoing network speed notifications.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE – To keep network speed monitoring active while the app is running in the background.
  • INTERNET – Required for normal network operations.

Data Sharing

Artlyx does not sell, share, rent, or disclose personal information to third parties.

Third-Party Services

The App may use services provided by Google Play and Android operating system components. These services are governed by their respective privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.
